Over the last three years, I have had the opportunity to participate in three projects.
Below, I will tell you more about them.

Hackaton

In this project, I had the opportunity to be a group leader. This first project was an introduction to technology, especially PowerApps, where the application was developed. Specifically, my application was a directory that would contain information about different tourist destinations in the country, where users could also calculate the budget for travelling to a certain place they chose. It would also have an integrated chatbot that would help users with any information they needed. I also had the opportunity to be in charge of the interactivity of the page so that everything would work.

Expo de logros

This was the second of the projects in which I had the opportunity to participate. Specifically, I was in the programming area, sometimes in back-end and sometimes in front-end. Here we developed a website from scratch. Our theme was events, and we were facilitators so that people offering their services for parties or events could do so from our website, allowing customers to search for them and buy their services. We also had extra tools, such as templates for events, quotes, and mixed reality to view your event in real time. I learned a lot from this project, but what stands out most is the teamwork.

Expotech

This is the latest project I participated in recently. Here, as in the previous project, we developed a website, but this one was more business plan-oriented. The website was about car maintenance, connecting mechanics with drivers. In addition, the website offered various tools such as a map of nearby mechanics, help with car maintenance reminders, and appointments with mechanics. Here, I worked in the front-end area.
