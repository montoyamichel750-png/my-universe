Volunteer

TECHO


Puesta de pilotes


During this volunteer experience, I participated in placing piles, which are the fundamental basis for building emergency housing. Throughout the day, we worked as a team measuring the land, checking the water level, digging to locate the piles, placing them in position, and filling them with earth to ensure they were firm and stable.

Fundación Tierra and UNO


Planta un árbol


I took part in the ‘Plant a Tree with UNO’ activity organised by the Fundación Tierra, which involved delivering trees to different service stations. During the day, we helped to distribute the plants. This experience allowed me to contribute directly to raising awareness about environmental care and promoting a more sustainable culture in our country.
