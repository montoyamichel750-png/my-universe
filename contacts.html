Personal epilogue

I look back, and I only see a little girl dreaming,
A girl who lived through everything,
Through ups and downs,
But who one day fell completely.

That girl wanted
To swim and to sing,
She wanted to see the world in new colors,
She wanted to live and to feel,
But not everything comes the way we want,
Sometimes we must walk through other lives to find what we really wish.

When we reach the lowest point,
We think it is the end,
That nothing better will come,
That everything is finished,
But sometimes we must believe a little more,
We don’t need a book of regrets to know we can change.

The only thing sure is this:
We must hold our love deep in the heart,
Because we have lived, faced problems, laughed, cried, felt anger,
And you know what?
We are still here, hug yourself and be thankful for all you have lived, even with pain.

Live, dream, create, and sing,
but never stop believing in your soul,
Because it will always be there, beside you,
Holding you, with the truest love there is.
