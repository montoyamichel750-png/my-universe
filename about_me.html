About Me

I'm Hazel Michelle Montoya Polio, I'm 18 years old and I live in Santa Tecla. I'm currently studying General High School at Centro Escolar Católico Alberto Masferrer, and I'm also part of the ¡Supérate! Fundación Poma program, where I'm receiving training in technology, English, and values. I enjoy participating in educational projects and volunteer work that help me grow as a person and contribute something positive. Throughout my learning journey, I've developed skills such as work planning, writing, leadership, mathematical reasoning, teamwork, responsibility, spatial reasoning, organization, and knowledge of JavaScript and HTML.


Learning, to me, is not just about completing tasks — it's about discovering, connecting, and transforming. I'm inspired by the details others overlook, by ideas that are felt before they're understood, and by spaces where the human blends with the academic. I enjoy observing, writing, creating, and sharing from a place that feels true to who I am. Every project is a chance to leave something of myself behind, something that speaks with clarity and soul. I don't want to be noise, I want to be echo: something that stays, something that touches.
